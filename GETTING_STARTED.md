## ğŸ¯ First Steps

### 1. Create Your First Todo
- Click the big blue **"Add New Todo"** button
- Or press `Ctrl+N` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+N` (Mac)
- Fill in:
  - **Title** (required): "Learn Productivity.AI features"
  - **Priority**: High
  - **Category**: Personal
- Click **"Create Todo"**

### 2. Explore the Interface

#### Top Action Bar
- **Kanban View** button: Switch to visual board layout
- **Analytics** button: View your productivity stats
- **Import/Export** button: Backup or restore your data
- **Shortcuts** button: See all keyboard shortcuts

#### Filter Bar
- **Search box**: Find todos by title, description, or tags
- **Status dropdown**: Filter by All, Active, Completed, or Archived
- **Category dropdown**: Filter by work, personal, etc.
- **Priority dropdown**: Filter by urgency level
- **Sort dropdown**: Choose how to order your todos

### 3. Try Advanced Features

#### Add a Subtask
1. Find your todo
2. Click **"Add Subtask"** button
3. Type: "Read the documentation"
4. Press Enter

#### Track Time
1. Click **"Start Timer"** on your todo
2. Work on the task
3. Click **"Stop Timer"** when done
4. Notice the time badge appears!

#### View Analytics
1. Click **"Analytics"** button at the top
2. See your completion rate, time tracked, and charts
3. Explore different metrics

#### Try Kanban View
1. Click **"Kanban View"** button (or press `Ctrl+K`)
2. See your todos organised by priority
3. Drag a card to a different column to change priority
4. Switch back with `Ctrl+K`

## ğŸ› Troubleshooting

### Todos Not Saving?
- Check that localStorage is enabled in your browser
- Check available storage space
- Try exporting and clearing data if needed

### Drag & Drop Not Working?
- Make sure "Manual Order" is selected in the Sort dropdown
- Refresh the page if needed
- Drag and drop works best on desktop

### Keyboard Shortcuts Not Working?
- Make sure you are not typing in an input field
- Try clicking outside any input first
- Press `?` to verify shortcuts
